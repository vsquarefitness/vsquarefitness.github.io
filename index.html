About Us

The V Square Fitness Gym is a unisex gym that houses an expansive selection of equipment, resources for a complete wellness experience. We provide a friendly, welcoming atmosphere for all members for all fitness levels from beginners to the PRO athletes. Whether your passion is, cardio, weight training, bodybuilding, powerlifting, Crossfit, or functional training; we have all the equipment and necessary machines you need for results. We offer the best training facilities, professional staff, and a wide selection of fitness classes and equipment.

Our membership options include:

  • Monthly Memberships: Choose from our selection of monthly plans, designed to cater to different training frequencies and preferences.
  • Quarterly Memberships: Benefit from discounted rates with our quarterly membership plans, perfect for those looking to make a longer commitment to their fitness journey.
  • Annual Memberships: Enjoy the best value and exclusive perks with our annual membership plans, designed for dedicated athletes seeking a long-term fitness solution.
  • In addition to our standard membership plans, we also offer Student & Corporate Discounts, Personal Training Packages, Drop-In Classes.
